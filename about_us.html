About Chikit360

Transforming pharmacy management with innovative technology solutions designed to enhance efficiency, improve patient care, and drive healthcare innovation.

Our Commitment to Pharmacy Excellence

Our Mission

To revolutionize pharmacy management through innovative technology solutions that enhance efficiency, reduce errors, and improve patient care. We strive to empower pharmacists with tools that allow them to focus on what matters most - their patients.

Our Vision

To become the leading pharmacy management solution provider globally, empowering pharmacies with smart technology for better healthcare delivery. We envision a future where every pharmacy operates at peak efficiency with minimal administrative burden.

Our Journey

Founded in 2023, Chikit360 emerged from a deep understanding of the challenges faced by modern pharmacies. Our team of healthcare professionals and technology experts came together with a shared goal: to create a comprehensive solution that would transform how pharmacies operate.

What started as a simple inventory management system has evolved into a complete pharmacy ERP solution, trusted by hundreds of pharmacies across India. Today, we process over 500,000 prescriptions monthly, helping pharmacies serve their communities more effectively.
